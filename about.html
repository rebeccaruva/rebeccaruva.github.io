ABOUT


Hi, I'm Bex!

I love games, toys, and anything that engages people in play. My current work builds upon this passion, creating functionality from everyday fun via design and technology.

I am a Master's student studying Interaction, Graphics and Design at Télécom Paris within the Institut Polytechnique de Paris. At Télécom Paris, I am currently working on research for data input physicalization.

Currently, I am seeking a 6-month R&D internship to start in January 2025.

In the past, I have worked at Google as an R&D User Experience Engineering Intern, at Parsons School of Design tutoring Design and Technology MFA and BFA students, and at Los Angeles Metro supervising high school interns within the in-house design studio.

I received my Bachelor's degree in Design and Technology, concentration in Games Design and Development, from Parsons School of Design.
